How much does apartment cleaning in Burgas cost?

The cost of apartment cleaning in Burgas depends on several factors: cleaning type (regular, deep, post-renovation), premise area, degree of contamination, need for additional services. The base price for regular cleaning starts from 50 leva for apartments up to 40 m². To get an accurate price, use our calculator or contact us by phone.

How long does apartment cleaning take?

Cleaning time depends on the area of the premises and the type of cleaning. On average, regular cleaning of a one-bedroom apartment takes 2-3 hours, deep cleaning – 4-6 hours, and post-renovation cleaning can take 6-8 hours. Our specialists work efficiently and thoroughly to provide quality results in optimal time.

What cleaning products are used during cleaning?

Our cleaning company in Burgas uses professional cleaning products from proven European manufacturers. We prefer eco-friendly products that effectively remove contamination but are safe for people, animals, and the environment. At the client's request, we can use hypoallergenic products or products provided by the customer.

Do I need to be present during cleaning?

Your presence is not required. You can give the keys to our manager and return to an already clean apartment. Our employees undergo careful selection, and we guarantee the safety of your property. If you prefer to be present during the first cleaning to provide necessary instructions, we will understand this.
